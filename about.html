About Us

Tour Ghana is a leading travel company rooted in the heart of West Africa, committed to delivering unforgettable experiences across the diverse and vibrant regions of Ghana. Since our founding, we’ve dedicated ourselves to sharing the beauty, culture, and warmth of our homeland with travelers from all walks of life.

Whether you're exploring the historical wonders of Cape Coast Castle, hiking the canopy walkway in Kakum National Park, sailing across the tranquil waters of Lake Bosomtwe, or immersing yourself in the colorful energy of Accra’s markets — Tour Ghana brings every moment to life with authenticity and care.

Our team is made up of passionate Ghanaians who know every hidden trail, local story, and cultural tradition. We go beyond typical tours — we connect you with real people, unforgettable places, and true experiences. With a focus on sustainability and community impact, we ensure that every journey not only supports the traveler, but also uplifts the locals who make it special.

At Tour Ghana, we believe travel should educate, inspire, and empower. That’s why our mission goes beyond sightseeing: we aim to help visitors discover the true soul of Ghana while supporting local artisans, guides, and communities.

Join us, and let’s go beyond the map — where every path leads to culture, every moment tells a story, and every tour feels like home.

Explore. Discover. Adventure — with Tour Ghana.
